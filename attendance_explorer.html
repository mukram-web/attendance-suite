How to read this

Unique viewers — distinct people who attended a session (Zoom's dedup). Used here as “attendance”.
Join events — total connections, counting re-joins; always higher than unique viewers.
Show-up % — unique viewers ÷ that session's Zoom registrant count. Runs high for these committed cohorts.
Avg watch — mean minutes attended per unique viewer, capped at the session length.
Stayed 80%+ — share of viewers present for at least 80% of the session runtime.
FFA retention — of everyone at Day 1, how many returned every day through Day N.
Source: Zoom webinar attendee reports (31 files across 7 batches). Host, panelist and Be10X team rows excluded from engagement and retention. Figures are as reported by Zoom; registrant counts are per-occurrence.
